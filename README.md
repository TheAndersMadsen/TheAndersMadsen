## About Me

I build software and infrastructure that make work faster, more reliable, and easier to automate.

My interests sit at the intersection of:

- AI agents and real-world automation
- infrastructure, routing, and backend systems
- marketing tech and attribution pipelines
- self-hosting, homelabs, and local AI
- security research and understanding how systems break

I started out building sneaker bots.  
That turned into a deeper interest in distributed systems, automation, performance, and web infrastructure under pressure.

## What I Work On

- Building internal tools and automations
- Designing robust infrastructure for production workflows
- Experimenting with local and hybrid LLM setups
- Integrating APIs, dashboards, and messaging systems
- Optimizing systems for speed, reliability, and lower human overhead
